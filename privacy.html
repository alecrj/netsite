üîê Your Privacy is Our Priority

At ResellAI, we believe your data belongs to you. We've built our platform with privacy-by-design principles to keep your information secure and give you complete control.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address and name when you create an account
  • Profile preferences and settings
  • Subscription and billing information

Item Data

  • Photos you upload for AI analysis
  • Product descriptions and pricing data
  • eBay listing information and sales data
  • Inventory tracking and performance metrics

Technical Data

  • Device information and app usage analytics
  • Error logs and crash reports (anonymized)
  • Performance data to improve our AI models

2. How We Use Your Information

  • AI Analysis: To identify items and suggest pricing
  • eBay Integration: To create and manage your listings
  • Service Improvement: To enhance our AI models and features
  • Support: To provide customer service and technical assistance
  • Communication: To send important updates and feature announcements

üö´ What We DON'T Do

We never sell your personal data to third parties. We never share your item photos with anyone except for AI processing. We never send spam or marketing emails without your consent.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

eBay Integration

When you connect your eBay account, we share necessary listing information with eBay to post your items. This is done securely through eBay's official API.

AI Processing

We use secure, privacy-compliant AI services to analyze your photos. Images are processed in encrypted environments and are not stored permanently by AI providers.

Service Providers

We work with trusted partners for:

  • Cloud hosting and data storage (encrypted)
  • Payment processing (PCI compliant)
  • Customer support tools
  • Analytics (anonymized data only)

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: SOC 2 compliant hosting environment
  • Data Backup: Encrypted, geographically distributed backups

5. Your Privacy Rights

Data Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we have about you.

Data Correction

You can update or correct your personal information at any time.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data.

Data Portability

You can export your data in a common format.

Marketing Opt-out

You can unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Item Photos: Deleted after AI processing (within 24 hours)
  • Sales Data: Retained for tax and business purposes (7 years max)
  • Support Data: Retained for 3 years for service improvement
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and retained for product development

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal, privacy-friendly tracking:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for app functionality
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage data to improve the app
  • Preferences: To remember your settings

You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

ResellAI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers, including Standard Contractual Clauses and adequacy decisions.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification. The updated policy will be posted on our website with the revision date.
